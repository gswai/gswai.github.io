What is Global Startup Weekend AI (GSWAI)?


GSWAI is a global initiative that started in Paris in 2016, and after a success, it went global to make artificial intelligence technologies more spread world wide, more diverse, inclusive, and impactful by removing barriers. We are boosting and connecting SW communities everywhere to organize their own SWAI and empower their ecosystems to tackle AI.

GSWAI in Numbers

Organize a Startup Weekend AI (#SWAI)

On the weekends of Global Startup Weekend AI end of 2018, organize a Startup Weekend Artificial Intelligence in your community! Startup Weekend is a 54-hour event designed to provide superior experiential education for technical and non-technical entrepreneurs, students and everybody interested in the entrepreneurial experience. The weekend event is centered on action, innovation, and education. Beginning on Friday night with an idea pitching session and continuing throughout the weekend with testing, business model development, and basic prototype creation, Startup Weekend culminates on Sunday night with demo pitching in front of a panel of potential investors and local entrepreneurs.
