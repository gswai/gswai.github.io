What is Global Startup Weekend AI (GSWAI)?


Over 1000 entrepreneurs & researchers will come together for tens of events the two first weekends of November 30 and December 8, 2018 for 54 hours of intensive work on functional startup prototypes, presented to a panel of experts, investors and entrepreneurs on the Sunday evening.
