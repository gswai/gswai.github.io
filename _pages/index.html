Drive for impact with Applied Artificial Intelligence

Drive for impact with Applied AI

We need to be bold and progress not only in AI, but in society, using technology as a tool to bring us closer, move us forward faster, but more responsible. Progress cannot be made in isolation, so we thrive to bring as many regions as possible together, to build the future we ALL want to live in. This is why at GSWAI we believe that communities around the world need to locally engage their ecosystems, to educate on them on the theme, in order to democratize the AI concept and push them out of their comfort zone to build the better tomorrow we all want to live in. This global initiative is more than a simultaneous event in several cities around the world: it is federating a genuine community around artificial intelligence, democratising its use, and making it more diverse, inclusive and impactful by removing boundaries.

What is Global Startup Weekend AI (GSWAI)?

GSWAI is a global volunteer-led initiative that started in Paris in 2016 it went global in 2017 to make artificial intelligence technologies more spread world wide, more diverse, inclusive, and impactful by removing barriers. We are boosting and connecting SW communities everywhere to organize their own SWAI and empower their ecosystems to tackle AI.

Tens of volunteer organisers are helping their local communities in emerging and developed countries to embrace their inner entrepreneur, and adopt new AI technologies to reply to world challenges.

Get involved

3 incredible ways to get involved

On the weekends of Global Startup Weekend AI end of 2018, organize a Startup Weekend Artificial Intelligence in your community! Startup Weekend is a 54-hour event designed to provide superior experiential education for technical and non-technical entrepreneurs, students and everybody interested in the entrepreneurial experience. The weekend event is centered on action, innovation, and education. Beginning on Friday night with an idea pitching session and continuing throughout the weekend with testing, business model development, and basic prototype creation, Startup Weekend culminates on Sunday night with demo pitching in front of a panel of potential investors and local entrepreneurs. Participants are challenged with building functional startups during the event and are able to collaborate with like-minded individuals.

Make your idea a reality. Join thousands of entrepreneurs around the world for the most exciting two weekends of the year and connect with the most qualified global mentors.
Get access to resources and connections such as pre-event workshops, remote mentoring, and live sessions with global communities during the weekend!
