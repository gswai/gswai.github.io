On the weekends of 23 and 30 of november the Global Startup Weekend AI will go life in 15 cities. The weekend event is centered on action, innovation, and education. Beginning on Friday night with an idea pitching session and continuing throughout the weekend with testing, business model development, and basic prototype creation, Startup Weekend culminates on Sunday night with demo pitching in front of a panel of potential investors and local entrepreneurs. Participants are challenged with building functional startups during the event and are able to collaborate with like-minded individuals.



Global Perks

Global AI Summit in Paris, 2019

4 Team Leaders of 4 winning teams of the Global AI Edition will be chosen and invited to Paris for two days of bootcamp & networking in 2019. Dates to be determined (April to May).

Here's how this is going to happen:

  1. 14 Local Startup Weekends AI will take place around the world during the last two weekends of November (23th and 30th)
  2. Our Global Jury will pick 4 Global Winners from 4 different cities
  3. Announcement will be made on the 5th of December
  4. Team Leaders of the 4 teams and one organizer from those 4 cities will come to Paris for the Global AI Summit in April-Mai 2019

Accomodation & flights will be offered by the Global AI team.

Next AI pre-qualification to accelerator & founder program

Delivered in Montréal and Toronto, NextAI is an accelerator and founder development program for early or idea stage AI-enabled startups. NextAI is for exceptional teams and individuals from around the world who are commercializing AI research and want to build global companies.

The winning team in each city will pre-qualify for NextAI's individual application round and will be able to directly apply as a team or join the pool of finalists to find a team.

Snips kit for Makers & Iot projects

The Maker Kit includes hardware components specially selected by Snips to easily build a powerful AI Voice Assistant, develop skills, and deploy to your connected device. (all the info you need about the kit here :).

Techstars AI Montreal mentorship

MD & Program Manager of the Techstars Montréal Accelerator Program will mentor the 3 winning teams for 1h per month, for three months prior to the Global AI Summit

Google cloud computing

  • We offered a free google cloud tier to all participants
  • For the Five finalists receive $20K in GCP Credits. Winner gets $100K in GCP Credits.
